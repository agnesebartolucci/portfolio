Story Posters for Generali

Generali, an Italian insurance company, celebrated its 190th anniversary in 2021 by commissioning a series of posters to depict its history through innovative milestones. Collaborating with their team, we extracted core elements from archival data and visualized each story using data visualization, graphic elements, historic photographs, documents, and illustrations. The result is a cohesive series of posters, unified by a distinctive visual style, providing a multifaceted insight into the company's history through data, images, and text.

2021 — Professional project, done at Accurat

Team: Marco Bernardi, Davide Grimoldi

Client: Generali Group

Skills: Data Visualization Design, Information Design, Graphic Design

My contribution: in this project, my main focus was on data visualization. I created visualizations for each poster, including those independent of the main content and those embedded within it. Additionally, I assisted in creating compositions and vector illustrations. My internship concluded before the project's completion, so the series comprises more posters, that were handled by others.
