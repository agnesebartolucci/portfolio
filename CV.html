Born in the hills of Urbino, living by the seaside in Helsinki.

Currently, I am a doctoral student at the Department of Art and Media at Aalto University. My research interests encompass information design, feminism, gender studies, digital humanities, internet and social studies, and particularly their intersections. I believe design can actively contribute to societal change, and I view research as the most effective means to achieve this. My PhD research focuses on exploring how feminist theory can influence design and archival practices.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am a visual designer with professional experience in information and data visualization, visual design, UX/UI, and editorial design. I excel in managing complex projects from concept to delivery, crafting cohesive visual identities, and devising innovative design solutions. I am actively seeking freelance collaborations to develop my skills further, take on new challenges, and continue growing as a designer.
