This Is Real, This Is Me

I am Agnese Bartolucci, a M. Sc. graduate with honours in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano. I am passionate, determined enough to be almost stubborn, and curious. Thanks to this latter, I like to do research on the topic I face before thinking of an adequate solution. Instead, thanks to the university, I learned the importance of working in a team to achieve a more complex result. In these years, I also acquired new competencies and improved myself with open-mindedness. Currently, I am based in Urbino, Italy. Anyway, I am looking for new challenges to test myself, and, of course, to move out again from my parent's house.

I love cats, Dr. Spencer Reid, good Harry Potter fan fiction, and articles about random shit that allow me to say: "You know, I've read an article…" at least three times a day.

I am interested in feminism and gender studies. In my independent work I like to explore the intersections between technology, internet and society because I think that design can actively contribute to bring change in society.

PS: If you are a coder, please don't look at the mess I wrote to make up this website.
