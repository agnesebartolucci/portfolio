HI!

I am Agnese Bartolucci, a doctoral student in the Aalto Visual Communication Design Research Group, at the Department of Art and Media of Aalto University. In my research, I am working on how to diversify digital archives through an intersectional feminist approach. I am passionate, determined, and curious. I value the importance of working in a team to achieve a more complex result. I am committed to acquiring new competencies and improving myself with open-mindedness. Currently, I am based in Helsinki, Finland. Anyway, I am always looking forward to new challenges and collaboration opportunities.

I love cats ✦, Dr. Spencer Reid, good Harry Potter and Stranger Things fan fiction (#EddieMunsonLives ✦), and articles about random shit that allow me to say: "You know, I've read an articleâ€¦" at least three times a day.

I am interested in feminism and gender studies. In my research and independent work, I like to explore the intersections between technology, the internet and society because I think that design can actively contribute to bringing changes in society.
