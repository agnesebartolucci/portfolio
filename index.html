Hi! I'm Agnese Bartolucci

☞ Visual Designer & PhD Candidate

Born in the hills of Urbino, living by the seaside in Helsinki.

I am a visual designer with professional experience in information and data visualization, UX/UI, and print design. I excel in managing complex projects from concept to delivery, crafting cohesive visual identities, and devising innovative design solutions that are accessible yet visually pleasing.

I am also a PhD candidate at Aalto University, exploring how intersectional feminism can influence digital archives. My research interests encompass information design, feminism, gender studies, digital humanities, internet and social studies, and particularly their intersections.

I love cats ✦, Dr. Spencer Reid, good Harry Potter and Stranger Things fan fiction, and random articles that allow me to say: "You know, I've read an article…" at least once a day.

↘ My services
I am available as a freelance visual designer. My core services include:
  1. Data Visualization and Information Design: I have extensive experience in producing both digital experiences with interactive visualizations and printable reports with readable graphs. My goal is to communicate data in a clear, consistent, and engaging manner.
  2. UX/UI and Interaction Design: I have experience in designing digital products with seamless user experience and unique visual identity. I can develop and maintain tailored visual assets for websites and apps, ensuring an enjoyable user experience.
