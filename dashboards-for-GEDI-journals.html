Dashboards for GEDI journals

GEDI Gruppo Editoriale S.p.A. is an Italian multimedia company active in printing, digital communication, radio, and television. I was responsible for designing dashboards and infographic content for their publications, particularly La Repubblica and its inserts. The goal was to offer readers clear overviews on specific topics.The newspaper team set graphic guidelines for visual consistency. Thus, each dashboard posed the challenge of creating novel graphs, visualizations, and graphics to ensure variety and engagement.

2021 — Professional project, done at Accurat

Supervisor: Marco Bernardi

Client: GEDI Editorial Group

Skills: Data Visualization Design, Information Design, Graphic Design, Editorial Design

The dashboards cover various topics corresponding to their respective inserts. "Salute" focuses on health-related news, research, and updates. "Green and Blue" tackles climate, biodiversity, energy, mobility, economy, and technology. "Cibo" centers on food, while "DLui" is a magazine tailored for men.

I handled the design of these commissions independently. The content was curated by the assigned journalists, while the layout guidelines were provided by the team at "la Repubblica" and Paper Paper Studio.
