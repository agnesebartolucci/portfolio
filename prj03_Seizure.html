Seizure

Epilepsy is an extremely diffused neurological disease. Despite this, epilepsy is still not completely understood due to its difficulty explaining and predicting some of its manifestations. The poster generator, written in Kotlin using the software rndr.studio, explores the topic displaying a collection of direct witnesses. These statements, made by people who explain what they feel during a seizure, were analyzed and divided into six metaphors that are visually represented by the alteration of the text. The other elements of the posters depict some symptoms such as spasms, loss of muscular control, loss of consciousness, and the perception of electric shock.

The poster generator highlights how each seizure is different from the others, giving the user the possibility to regulate the intensity of the alterations.

↑ The six identified metaphors are crawling, electricity, oppression; overpower; space, and vortex. Each element in the poster represents a specific symptom. The white color represents the progressive loss of consciousness, and the blue color symbolises the electricity due to the overload of brain activity.

MY CONTRIBUTION
This project was born during a one-week workshop. So my group has been working under quite a lot of pressure. I have mainly contributed to the research and concept part, and I have programmed some of the functions to edit the text.
