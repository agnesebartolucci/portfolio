Data Visualization, Infographic, Editorial Design

Dataviz for GEDI journals

GEDI Gruppo Editoriale S.p.A. is an Italian multimedia company active in the printing, digital communication, radio, and television sectors. Among other things, it is the publisher of the newspaper la Repubblica and its inserts and attachments. For some of these publications were designed dashboards or other infographic content, to give the reader an immediate overview of a specific topic. The team of the newspapers established graphic guidelines and palettes to maintain visual consistency. Therefore, the challenge of each dashboard was to create new graphs, visualizations, and graphics to create a product that is always different and interesting.

The dashboards explore different topics, in line with the inserts they were intended for. “Salute” deals with news, research, and updates from the world of health. “Green and Blue” deals with climate, biodiversity, energy, mobility, economy, technology. “Cibo” is a new insert about food. Finally, “DLui” is a magazine dedicated to men.

I took care of the design of these commissions alone. Instead, the contents were studied by the journalists in charge, while the layout guidelines were handled by the team of "la Repubblica" and Paper Paper Studio.
