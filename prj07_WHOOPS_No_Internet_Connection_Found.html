WHOOPS! No Internet Connection Found

→ website
→ download the visualization

An internet shutdown is a disruption of internet communications caused by technical problems or, more often, a government decision to exert control over some specific events. The visualization shows the location, quantity, duration, and causes (both suspected and declared) of the shutdowns. The infographic includes a further level of interpretation, which is the democracy index of each country, to understand if there is a connection between the type of government and the Internet shutdowns.

"WHOOPS! No internet connection found" only shows the total outages in 2018: a total internet shutdown occurs when all internet services are completely blocked. The missing countries did not have any internet shutdown or did not release any data about it.

↑ The infographic was designed through various steps, starting from a grid that served as a basis for arranging the different modules representing each shutdown.

MY CONTRIBUTION
I helped arrive at the final result through experimentation with various visualization models and visual styles. I also dealt with the research, analysis, and organization of a part of the data.
