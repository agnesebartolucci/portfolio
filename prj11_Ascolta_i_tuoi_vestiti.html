Bachelor Thesis, Editorial Design, Communication Campaign

Ascolta i tuoi vestiti

Share is a social reality in business in the tertiary sector that sells high-quality second-hand clothes from Italy and foreign countries. The thesis explains the communication campaign designed for this business during the six-months long course Final Synthesis Design Studio. A research on the brand-awareness put the basis to the project by analyzing the identity of the shop, the life cycle of second-hand clothes, and the people's opinion through online surveys and direct interviews. Then was a three-phased campaign designed to attract more people to the shops and the brand philosophy. The first phase raises awareness on the donation of old clothes; the second phase tries to change the harmful idea toward second-hand clothing. Finally, the third phase invites the user to enter the store.
