Spring Off the Wall

Vans is a standout brand in the sportswear realm, rooted in values of youth, authenticity, and individual style, partly owing to its association with tabletop sports. With a goal to inspire customers to think differently, they define their approach as "Off the Wall." To embody these values, a workshop for students was conducted by the Vans EMEA Team and the Politecnico di Milano, aiming to design an event for Milan Design Week 2018.

The outcome materialized as a space divided into four distinct areas. The first served as an immersion into the Vans world, while the others were themed around sensations experienced by skaters during practice (vertigo, balance, and speed). A team of Communication Design students, myself included, was tasked with creating the event's communication system, amalgamating ideas presented during the workshop's initial week. We developed a campaign centered on three symbols inspired by skating and each corresponding sensation.

2018 — University and professional project

Team: Leonardo Codamo, Francesca Dell'Orto, Andrea Folini, Davide Formenti, Stefano Maffoni, Carlo Ottaviani, Valentina Pagano, Eleonora Rossetti, Marco Valli

Faculty: Venanzio Arquilla, Giulia Gerosa, Umberto Tolino

In collaboration with Vans EMEA Team

Skills: Communication Campaign Design, Graphic Design, Exhibition Design

My contribution: during the initial week of the workshop, I assisted in conceptualizing the three experiences we proposed for the event. However, my primary role was in designing the communication campaign and establishing the graphic identity. As the project progressed, I continued to contribute to refining the communication campaign, aiding in the integration and coherence of ideas from various projects. Lastly, I took on the role of project manager, overseeing communication with the company.

For the communication campaign, we created three symbols inspired by skating, both in 2D and 3D, representing each sensation. Each feeling was associated with a skate legend and Vans ambassador: Tony Alva for balance, Steve Caballero for vertigo, and Christian Hosoi for speed. These symbols formed the core elements of our artifacts, beginning with the poster and extending to the three flyers. Additionally, we designed red-colored stickers for a guerrilla marketing campaign in Milan, complementing those featuring the ambassadors that were distributed in shops.

The event's details were disseminated through Vans' official channels and prominent platforms of Milan Design Week, including Fuorisalone.it.

The Vans EMEA Team granted us autonomy in our decision-making, and this collaboration culminated in an active presence during the event.
